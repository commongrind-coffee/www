Your Monthly Journey

Each month follows a rhythm designed for exploration, experimentation, and genuine connection.

01

Shipment Arrives

Your box contains:

  • Three curated coffees (50g each)
  • Detailed recipe cards per coffee
  • Grind range, ratio, temperature
  • Pour structure & timing
  • What to pay attention to
02

Make the Reference

Week one: brew all three coffees following the recipes exactly. This creates your baselineâ€”the shared experience that anchors all comparison.

Taste carefully. Take notes. Notice the differences between origins, roasts, and processing methods.

03

Experiment

Weeks 2-3: Now the real exploration begins. Adjust one variable at a time:

  • Grind finer or coarser
  • Change water temperature
  • Adjust your ratio
  • Vary pour technique

Document what changes and why.

04

Share & Discuss

Share your findings with the community. Reference the baseline before describing your changes. Compare notes. Debate. Learn.

Week 4: Monthly virtual tasting where members discuss discoveries in real-time.

Required Gear

No proprietary equipment needed. Just quality basics that eliminate variables and let the coffee speak.

Pour-Over Dripper

Ceramic V60 or similar cone dripper. Size 02 recommended. Consistent, repeatable, and accessible.

Hand Grinder or Electric

Capable of medium-fine grind settings. Manual (Timemore C2) or electric (Baratza Encore) both work.

Digital Scale

0.1g accuracy minimum. Built-in timer preferred. Essential for consistent ratios.

Gooseneck Kettle

Temperature control preferred. Allows precise pour control and consistency.

Filters

Paper filters matched to your dripper. We'll specify which for each month's recipes.

Timer

If your scale doesn't have one. Precision matters for replicable results.
